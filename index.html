Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

Do you handle Dubai Municipality approvals?
Yes, we provide end-to-end services including all necessary permits from DM, Trakhees, and DEWA.
Are your projects Al Sa'fat compliant?
Absolutely. Sustainability is a core value, and we implement Dubai's Green Building System in every project.
How long does the DEWA connection process take?
The timeline varies by project size, but we manage the entire application and inspection process to ensure timely activation.
Do you provide soil testing for coastal construction?
Yes, specialized geotechnical surveys are mandatory for projects in areas like Palm Jumeirah to ensure foundation stability.
What are the Al Sa'fat green building requirements?
It focuses on resource efficiency and environmental health, which we integrate through solar orientation and high-efficiency HVAC.
Is your team certified for Trakhees approvals?
Yes, we are fully licensed to work within JAFZA and other DP World managed developments requiring Trakhees permits.
Can you assist with District Cooling integration?
We coordinate directly with Empower or Emicool for seamless connection to the regional cooling networks.
